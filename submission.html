Submission

Papers must be submitted online by June 19, 2025, exclusively exclusively via CMT platform. Submissions should adhere to the IEEE standard two-column format. The first page must include the title of the paper, the authors’ names and affiliations, an abstract, and keywords, all centered below the top margin. Each paper is limited to six pages, with the option to add up to two additional pages for a fee. The page count must include all illustrations and references.

All submitted papers will undergo a double blind review process managed by the Review and Publication committees in collaboration with the Technical Program Committee (TPC) chairs. Authors will be informed of the review outcomes by August 21, 2025. Final versions of accepted papers must be submitted by September 18, 2025.

Authors are strongly encouraged to use the official IEEE Templates available in both MS Word and LaTeX formats to ensure compatibility with the IEEE Xplore® Digital Library. To learn more about formatting of papers for IEEE IES conferences, please visit this page Paper Format for Conferences. Submitted manuscripts will be checked for PDF compliance and originality using the CrossCheck. Only original, unpublished work is accepted.

Accepted papers must be presented during the conference. Final manuscripts must be accompanied by a completed registration form and proof of full payment. Presented and accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion in IEEE Xplore, but inclusion is not guaranteed and is subject to meeting IEEE Xplore’s scope and quality requirements.
