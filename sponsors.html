Our Sponsors and Partners

UMMTO

The University Mouloud Mammeri Tizi-Ouzou (UMMTO) is a prominent Algerian university offering diverse academic programs and engaging in significant research activities. Located in Tizi-Ouzou, UMMTO is committed to providing quality education and fostering innovation.

ATRST

The Agency for Thematic Research in Science and Technology (ATRST) supports scientific research and technological innovation in Algeria. Its mission is to foster collaboration and advance research in key scientific fields to drive national development.

DGRSDT

The Directorate-General for Scientific Research and Technological Development (DGRSDT) is responsible for promoting and coordinating scientific research in Algeria. It plays a key role in fostering innovation and supporting national development through research initiatives.

American Polytechnic Institute (API)

The American Polytechnic Institute (API) is committed to providing high-quality education and training in engineering and technology. API focuses on fostering innovation, research, and industry collaboration to equip students with the necessary skills to excel in modern technological fields.

IEEE PES Power & Energy Society

The IEEE Power & Energy Society (PES) Algeria Section is a chapter of IEEE PES, a leading organization focused on the development and application of technology in the electric power and energy industry. PES provides resources, networking opportunities, and technical expertise to professionals working to advance sustainable energy solutions.

IEEE Power Electronics Society

The IEEE Power Electronics Society (PELS) Algeria Section is a chapter of IEEE PELS, dedicated to promoting technological advancements in power electronics. PELS supports research, education, and collaboration among professionals working on power conversion, energy efficiency, and related fields to drive innovation in modern power systems.

IEEE Algeria Section

The IEEE Algeria Section is part of the global IEEE organization, focusing on advancing technology for humanity. It brings together professionals in electrical and electronic engineering, computer science, and related fields to foster innovation, education, and collaboration within Algeria.
