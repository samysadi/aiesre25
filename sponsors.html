Our Sponsors

Terra Source

Terra Source Institute is dedicated to developing local capacity for sustainable and potable water supplies. Their mission includes empowering communities to assess water needs, protecting resources, and disseminating information about water contamination globally.

Aurestech

Aurestech specializes in providing innovative technological solutions to enhance industrial efficiency and productivity. Their expertise lies in developing cutting-edge software and hardware tailored to meet the unique needs of various industries.

UMMTO

The University Mouloud Mammeri Tizi-Ouzou (UMMTO) is a prominent Algerian university offering diverse academic programs and engaging in significant research activities. Located in Tizi-Ouzou, UMMTO is committed to providing quality education and fostering innovation.

CRTSE

The Research Center in Semiconductor Technology and Energetics (CRTSE) focuses on advancing semiconductor technologies and energy solutions. Their research contributes to the development of sustainable and efficient energy systems.

CRTI

The Research Center in Industrial Technologies (CRTI) is dedicated to advancing industrial technologies through research and development. They focus on areas such as non-destructive testing, welding techniques, signal processing, and materials development to enhance industrial processes and innovation.

CDER

The Renewable Energy Development Center (CDER) is committed to promoting and researching renewable energy solutions. Their work encompasses various renewable energy sources, aiming to support sustainable development and energy independence.

IEEE Advancing Technology for Humanity

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. With a global network of engineers, scientists, and professionals, IEEE fosters innovation in various fields, including electronics, computing, and communications.

IEEE PES Power & Energy Society

The IEEE Power & Energy Society (PES) is a leading organization focused on the development and application of technology in the electric power and energy industry. PES provides resources, networking opportunities, and technical expertise to professionals working to advance sustainable energy solutions.

IEEE Power Electronics Society

The IEEE Power Electronics Society (PELS) is dedicated to promoting technological advancements in power electronics. PELS supports research, education, and collaboration among professionals working on power conversion, energy efficiency, and related fields to drive innovation in modern power systems.

IEEE Algeria Section

The IEEE Algeria Section is part of the global IEEE organization, focusing on advancing technology for humanity. It brings together professionals in electrical and electronic engineering, computer science, and related fields to foster innovation, education, and collaboration within Algeria.
